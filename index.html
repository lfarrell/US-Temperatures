Is the World Warming?

Climate change doesn't mean it's always hot outside, or that the world will warm uniformly. Notice that though the world hasn't had a below average monthly temperature since 1984. The average temperature of the United States was below 20th century historical averages as recently as February 2015. Note: World temperature anomalies have been converted from Celsius to Fahrenheit
